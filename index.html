WHO IS ROY MUBIRU

Roy Mubiru, born on 12th December 1977. Is a South African based athlete and founder of RM powerlifting. Roy is Uganda's number one heavyweight powerlifter and world powerliting champion with numerous titles to his name.

FOR More

BACKGROUND

Born and raised in Uganda in a small town, Roy moved to South Africa as a boxer but later went on  to pursue his powerliting dream in 2008 instead, years later his career took off and he participated in numerous competitions around the world, and he never looked back ever since.

CAREER

Since 2008 Roy Mubiru participated in many competitions around the world. Including the Revolution powerliting championships, The Gauteng North gym oxygen powerlifting championships, musclemania powerlifting championships, in USA, South Africa, Thailand, Great Britain just to name a few and not forgetting his very own annual Roy Mubiru powerlifting and bodybuilding championships in uganda.

BIOGRAPHY

Roy Mubiru, born on 12th December 1977 in Nalyaa Village Wakiso District, Uganda. Son to the late Sempanda Galiko Samuel  and Nakabugo Justine, Roy is the 2nd Born of 6 boys. He is Married to  Catherine Nakate and a father to Dion Nagadya mubiru, Tamia Nambi Mubiru, Troy Reginald Mubiru, Timon Reynalds Mubiru.

CAREER

in the year 1988 Roy started playing soccer in primary school util 1995, Roy was then started boxing at KBC boxing club in down town kampala, Lukanga boxing club his competions were held at Lugogo Indoors Arena as a amature fighter. In 2000 Roy moved to South Africa as a Boxer and turned into a professional Boxer until 2006 when he was introduced to powerlifting by his than manager Rhyan till now he has never looked back and has competed in multiple competitions with numerous titles. With the success of his career Roy never forgot to give back to his country, by hosting a number of events in Uganda like the Annual Roy Mubiru powerlifting event at theatre La'bonita to promote powerlifting and bodybuilding in Uganda for the upcoming athletes.
